# Agentic Dev Days Stockholm 2026

**April, 2026 | Stockholm, Sweden | 300 Practitioners**

---

## The AI Software Development Lifecycle Revolution

The way we build software is fundamentally changing. AI is no longer just a feature we ship - it's reshaping how we write code, collaborate, and deliver value.

Agentic Dev Days Stockholm brings together 300 developers, engineers, and technical leaders for a full-day deep dive into the practices, tools, and mindsets defining AI-native development. This conference scales up our monthly meetups - which consistently gather 100 participants - to create a comprehensive gathering where the entire Nordic AI development community can connect, learn, and advance the field together.

---

## What We'll Explore

### Agentic Development Environments

Moving beyond copilots to AI agents that understand context, execute tasks, and collaborate as team members in your development workflow.

### AI Security

Protecting AI systems from attacks, securing training data, and using AI to enhance security practices across the development lifecycle.

### The AI-Native Developer

Essential skills, practices, and mental models for thriving in an AI-augmented development world.

## A Community-Driven Conference

Agentic Dev Days Stockholm is a self-organizing, practitioner-focused event. We're built on partnerships and ticket sales, which means we can focus entirely on creating value for the community and keep ticket prices accessible to all practitioners.

### Our Philosophy

- We build real connections for further developing the field
- Knowledge sharing over product presentations
- Authentic community building, not lead generation
- Engineers talking to engineers about real challenges and solutions

## Why Partner With Us?
